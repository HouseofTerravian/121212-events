Privacy Policy

How 121212.events collects and uses information.

Effective: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an event through our form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your organization name (if provided)
  • Event details (name, date, venue, description, ticket URL)
  • Optional social media handles

When you browse the Site, we may collect standard analytics data through third-party analytics tools, including page views, referral sources, and general geographic region. We do not collect personally identifiable information from browsers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through event submissions is used exclusively to:

  • Review and process your event listing request
  • Notify you of the status of your submission
  • Display approved event details on the Site
  • Contact you about corrections or updates to your listing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

Event submission contact information is retained as long as the listing remains active on the Site. You may request removal of your contact information while retaining the event listing by emailing us at the address below.

4. Cookies

121212.events may use minimal cookies or local storage for analytics and site performance. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

The Site may be hosted through Vercel and may use third-party analytics tools. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share personal data with them beyond what is necessary for site operation.

6. Your Rights

You may request that we correct or delete personal information you submitted. Contact us at privacy@121212.events with your request and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of any revisions.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: privacy@121212.events
